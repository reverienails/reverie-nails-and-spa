Our Philosophy

A space where
skill meets warmth.

At Reverie, we blend exceptional quality and technical skill with everyday care and glamorous touches. Our technicians are encouraged to build real connections — getting to know you as you get to know them.

We envision Reverie as the community’s favorite “watering hole” — a warm, welcoming space where old friends reconnect and new ones are made. Our open layout and signature circular nail table are designed to spark conversation and connection.

Whether you’re here for a luxurious pedicure or a quick set of nails, we hope you leave feeling relaxed, refreshed, and a little more connected.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer student or military discounts?
Yes — we offer special pricing every day for military, teachers, and students. Just mention it when you book.
What nail types do you offer?
Natural manicures, dipping powder, Acrylics, Solar Nails, French/ombre nails, Designs, Builder Gel, Gel-X and extra pampering if needed
Are drinks really complimentary?
Yes. Every guest receives complimentary beverages during their service — wine, rosé, mocktails, water, and more.
Do I need an appointment?
Appointments are highly recommended, especially on weekends. Walk-ins are welcome when space allows.
What should I expect during my first visit?
We’ll welcome you, go over your service, and make sure you’re comfortable. Our goal is for you to leave feeling relaxed and happy with your results.
Do you take walk-ins?
Yes, we accept walk-ins when our schedule allows. Booking ahead guarantees your preferred time and technician.
